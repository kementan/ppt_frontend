Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how Portal Pertanian Terintegrasi collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our Google API-based login and email access services. By using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

When you use our application and log in using Google API, we may collect the following information:

  1. Google Account Information: To provide authentication and access to email services, we may collect your Google account details, including your name and email address.

  2. Email Data: Our application may access your email data with your explicit permission, such as your email content and metadata.

  3. Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use our application and services, including log data, device information, and browser type.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  1. Authentication: To verify your identity and provide you with access to our services.

  2. Email Access: To enable the features that require access to your email data as authorized by you.

  3. Improvement and Development: To improve our application, diagnose technical issues, and develop new features.

  4. Communications: To send you important updates, notifications, and service-related communications.

Data Security

We take data security seriously and employ reasonable measures to protect your information. However, please note that no method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or with your explicit consent.

Your Choices

You can manage your privacy settings and revoke our access to your Google account at any time. You can also contact us to request the removal of your data from our systems.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, regulatory, or security reasons. We will notify you of any substantial changes through our application or website.

Contact Us
